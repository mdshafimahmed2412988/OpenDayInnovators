Postgraduate Courses

1. BSc Business Management

Overview: This course provides students with a strong foundation in business principles, leadership, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship.

Core Modules:

  • Business Environment and Strategy
  • Marketing and Consumer Behavior
  • Financial Accounting and Analysis
  • Human Resource Management
  • Entrepreneurship and Business Innovation

Career Opportunities:

  • Business Consultant
  • Marketing Manager
  • Financial Analyst
  • Project Manager

Why Choose This Course?

  • Real-world case studies and industry projects
  • Internship and placement opportunities
  • Accredited by professional business bodies

2. BA Marketing

Overview: A course designed to equip students with skills in digital marketing, branding, advertising, and consumer psychology.

Core Modules:

  • Digital Marketing and Social Media
  • Market Research and Analytics
  • Advertising and Brand Management
  • Consumer Psychology
  • International Marketing

Career Opportunities:

  • Digital Marketing Specialist
  • Advertising Executive
  • Brand Manager
  • Market Research Analyst

Why Choose This Course?

  • Hands-on experience with industry tools like Google Analytics
  • Live marketing campaigns and business case studies

3. BEng Mechanical Engineering

Overview: This course focuses on mechanical system design, thermodynamics, materials science, and manufacturing processes.

Core Modules:

  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Mechanics of Materials
  • Thermodynamics and Fluid Mechanics
  • Manufacturing Processes
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD)

Career Opportunities:

  • Mechanical Engineer
  • Automotive Engineer
  • Aerospace Engineer
  • Robotics Specialist

Why Choose This Course?

  • Access to state-of-the-art engineering labs
  • Accredited by professional engineering institutions

4. BEng Civil Engineering

Overview: This program covers structural design, construction management, environmental engineering, and sustainable development.

Core Modules:

  • Structural Analysis and Design
  • Construction Materials and Methods
  • Hydraulics and Water Resources
  • Sustainable Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering

Career Opportunities:

  • Civil Engineer
  • Structural Engineer
  • Construction Project Manager
  • Environmental Consultant

Why Choose This Course?

  • Hands-on training with engineering software like AutoCAD and Revit
  • Industry placements with construction firms

5. BSc Cybersecurity

Overview: This course provides students with the skills to protect systems and networks from cyber threats and hacking attempts.

Core Modules:

  • Computer Networks and Security
  • Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing
  • Cryptography and Data Protection
  • Cybersecurity Risk Management
  • Digital Forensics

Career Opportunities:

  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • Ethical Hacker
  • Information Security Consultant
  • Security Architect

Why Choose This Course?

  • Access to the latest cybersecurity labs and tools
  • Taught by industry professionals with hands-on hacking simulations

6. BSc Nursing

Overview: A professional course designed to train students in healthcare practices, patient care, and clinical procedures.

Core Modules:

  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Clinical Skills and Patient Care
  • Public Health and Epidemiology
  • Nursing Ethics and Legal Framework
  • Mental Health Nursing

Career Opportunities:

  • Registered Nurse
  • Community Health Nurse
  • Critical Care Nurse
  • Public Health Specialist

Why Choose This Course?

  • Approved by the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC)
  • Practical training in hospitals and healthcare facilities

7. LLB Law

Overview: This course provides students with a comprehensive understanding of legal principles, contract law, criminal law, and human rights.

Core Modules:

  • Introduction to Legal Systems
  • Contract and Tort Law
  • Criminal Law and Justice
  • Human Rights Law
  • Business and Corporate Law

Career Opportunities:

  • Lawyer (Solicitor or Barrister)
  • Legal Advisor
  • Corporate Counsel
  • Paralegal

Why Choose This Course?

  • Accredited by the Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA)
  • Opportunities for internships in law firms
