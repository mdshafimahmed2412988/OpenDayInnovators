Our History & Legacy

Founded in 1827 as the Wolverhampton Mechanics' Institute, the University of Wolverhampton has grown into a globally recognized institution with multiple campuses, cutting-edge research facilities, and a strong industry presence.

Our journey from a small technical college to a comprehensive university has been marked by innovation and expansion. In 1969, we became Wolverhampton Polytechnic, and in 1992, we were granted university status.

Today, we continue to build on our legacy of providing accessible, high-quality education that transforms lives and communities.
